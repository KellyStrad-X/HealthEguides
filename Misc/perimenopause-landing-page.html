Yes! If you're experiencing any of the symptoms and are between 35-55, this guide will help you identify patterns, understand what's happening, and know when to seek help. It's designed for women who suspect perimenopause as well as those already diagnosed.

Do I need to be on HRT to benefit from this guide?

Not at all! The playbook covers HRT extensively (including modern research), but also includes comprehensive lifestyle strategies, supplement guidance, and non-HRT treatment options. Many women manage perimenopause without HRT.

Will this replace seeing a doctor?

No—this guide is designed to help you work MORE effectively with your doctor. It gives you the knowledge and language to advocate for proper care, but always consult with a healthcare provider about your specific situation.

The guide includes specific conversation scripts AND red flags that indicate it's time to find a new provider. You'll also get resources for finding menopause specialists and telehealth options.
